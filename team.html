Cow

About Cow
Cow was our first residential ham, and also Rachelle’s motivation to start The Cosy Paws. Being the pickiest ham that Rachelle ever saw, Cow has set her en route to developing and sourcing for the best quality snacks & treats that the local market never had.

Butters

About Butters
Butters, our most camera shy ham, rarely appears in our photos & feed. However, Butters has the most expensive taste buds, which is the main reason why TCP only uses top ingredients, and never settles for less.

Ollie

About Ollie
Ollie was adopted back in Nov 2020. He was being kept with his siblings in a tiny carrier, and was even suspected to be inbred. Being only ~70 grams at 8 months old, Ollie had to see the vet repeatedly for several health problems. Ollie passed on 10 April 2021, but we still hold him dearest in our hearts.

Kayla

About Kayla
Kayla was rescued and adopted back in mid 2020. She is a glutton ham, and would crawl her way out from her deep burrows just to munch on the snacks given to her. She also absolutely loves burrowing, which makes her our burrowing inspector!

Kiko

About Kikko
Kayla’s elder sister, but appears to be much shyer than Kayla! Unlike most robos, Kiko is a super chill ham who gets very satisfied once she has her favourite hideout! However, we will still spot her zooming about her enclosure, especially after revamping it!

Patches

About Patches
Patches is our newest member to the family. She was being abandoned by her ex-owner due to aggression issues, but now stuck with a family that showers her with TLC!

Chewy

About Chewy
The biggest and most senior furkid in the family - Chewy was the furkid who started our homebaked noms, as she has a highly-sensitive stomach which resulted in most commercial treats not being suitable for her. Chewy is also the why most of our homebaked noms are safe & suitable for dogs too!
